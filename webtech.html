This is a Content-Sharing Platform.

  • Developed a website with a robust registration system featuring comprehensive validations and a secure login system, ensuring user data integrity and security.
  • Designed a dynamic content-sharing platform where users can create and interact with posts across a wide range of computer programming categories, including Java, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML, Ruby, and more.
  • Implemented user-centric features such as post management (creation, viewing, and deletion), user profile customization (including profile picture and password updates), and user-to-user interactions such as liking and commenting on posts.
  • Empowered users with full control over their accounts, allowing them to delete their accounts while ensuring automatic removal of all their associated posts, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Implemented seamless social interaction by integrating a Facebook plugin, enabling users to easily like, comment, and share posts, enhancing user engagement and social sharing capabilities on the platform.
