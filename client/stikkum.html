This is a US mortgage-based project.

  • Developed and managed ”Stikkum,” a US mortgage-based project that delivers custom alerts to users based on their specific requirements, with a focus on monetizing each alert.
  • Enabled unlimited sub-accounts and branch accounts for users while providing specialised access control, enhancing the project’s scalability and flexibility.
  • Successfully integrated multiple APIs, including BoomBoom, Zapier, ChatGPT, and others, to enhance user experience and increase project versatility.
  • Empower users to design custom templates and schedules based on alert responses and events, allowing for personalised behaviours in scheduled emails and SMS notifications, enhancing the project’s adaptability to individual user needs.
